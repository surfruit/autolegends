FAQ

How to avoid buying a 'pig in a poke' from the USA?

With Auto Legends USA, it’s easy. Our experts have bought over 2100 cars from the USA and can assess risks from photos. We avoid heavily damaged cars, sticking to those with minor, easily replaceable exterior damage that won’t affect performance. We also check service history with specialized tools for full transparency. With us, you get a great deal and a reliable ride.

What type of damage is best to choose at US auctions?

Damage type affects repair costs and value. Here’s what we recommend: 1. **Mechanical** — minor engine or transmission issues, cheap to fix. 2. **Body** — scratches or dents, as long as the frame’s intact. 3. **Airbags** — affordable to replace if no other damage. 4. **Flood** — profitable if water didn’t hit critical systems. 5. **Electrical** — small wiring issues, quick to repair. Avoid cars with frame damage or complex breakdowns.

How to pay for a car and delivery from the USA?

Due to SWIFT restrictions in Ukraine since 2022, we offer payments via Europe, the USA, or Georgia—fast and penalty-free. On Copart, IAAI, Manheim, fees are 2.5% of the car price + $150 per payment. Delivery is paid via international transfer, covering transport to the port and costs in Ukraine. We ensure a secure, swift process.

What guarantees do you provide?

We guarantee a turnkey price in Ukraine without repairs and the car’s integrity during delivery. You can also insure it—any damage en route gets fully covered for repairs.

Where’s the best place to buy cars in the USA?

Top auctions: **Copart & IAAI** — damaged cars, great for savings with repairs. **Manheim & ACV Auctions** — clean cars from dealers, low risk. **eBay Motors** — versatile, from damaged to pristine. We’ll find the best fit for you.
